Note for Users: Checking Soil Nutrients

To accurately predict suitable crops for your soil using our website, it's essential to know the soil's nutrient levels and pH. We recommend visiting your nearby agriculture department or local agricultural extension office to:

  1. Test Soil Nutrients: Determine levels of nitrogen (N), phosphorus (P), and potassium (K). These nutrients are critical for plant growth and crop selection.

  2. Measure Soil pH: Understand the acidity or alkalinity of your soil. pH affects nutrient availability and crop suitability.

Once you have this information, input the results into our website for tailored crop recommendations based on your soil's specific conditions.

For further assistance or guidance on soil testing, feel free to reach out to our support team.
